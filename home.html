Babulal Tarabai Institute of Research and Technology (BTIRT) is a private engineering college located at Sironja in Sagar district of Madhya Pradesh. It was established by Little Star Educational Society, Sagar in 2008. It is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Technological University Bhopal.
College offers undergraduate degree in engineering in five disciplines, a MBA program and MCA program. All the courses are approved by AICTE and DTE Govt. of MP. It is also an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Institution The mission of the BTIRT is to be one of the leading technical institutes disseminating globally acceptable education, effective industrial training and relevant research output Departments: BTIRT has following academic departments-

    2. Department of Business Administration
    3. Department of Electronics & Communications Engineering
    4. Department of Mechanical Engineering
    5. Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering
    6. Department of Computer Science and Engineering
